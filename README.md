# Superstore Sales Analysis

## Overview
Analyzed 9,800 sales transactions from a US superstore to identify 
key business insights across regions, categories, and customers.

## Business Questions Answered
1. Which region generates the most sales?
2. Which product category performs best?
3. Who are the top 10 customers by sales?
4. Which month has the highest sales?
5. Does shipping time affect sales amount?

## Key Insights
- **West region** leads in both order count and total revenue
- **South region** is weakest — needs marketing focus
- **Technology** generates highest revenue despite fewer orders
- **November** is peak sales month — driven by festival season
- **No correlation** between shipping days and sales amount

## Tools Used
- Python, Pandas, NumPy

## Dataset
- Source: Kaggle Superstore Dataset
- Rows: 9,800 | Columns: 18
