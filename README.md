# Superstore Sales Analysis

## Overview
Analyzed 9,800 sales transactions from a US superstore dataset to uncover business insights across regions, product categories, customer behavior, and sales trends using Python and Power BI.

## Business Questions Answered
1. Which region generates the highest sales?
2. Which product category performs best?
3. Who are the top 10 customers by sales?
4. Which month records the highest sales?
5. Does shipping time affect sales amount?

## Key Insights
- **West region** leads in both total sales and order volume
- **South region** shows the lowest performance and may require marketing improvements
- **Technology** category generates the highest revenue despite fewer orders
- **November** is the peak sales month due to seasonal demand
- **No significant correlation** found between shipping days and sales amount
